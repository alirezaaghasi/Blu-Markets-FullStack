# Blu Markets — Product Description (Locked v7.6)

## 1. What Blu Markets Is
Blu Markets is a **portfolio integrity system** for non-professional users operating under economic stress.
It is not a trading platform, not a speculative casino, and not a paternalistic robo-advisor.

Its purpose is to give users **agency with guardrails**: freedom to act, paired with friction only when actions threaten structural integrity.

## 2. Target User
Blu Markets is built for users who:
- Are not professional traders
- Live under high inflation and economic uncertainty
- Feel pressure to “do something” with their money
- Distrust both banks (loss of purchasing power) and exchanges (risk, complexity)

## 3. Core Mental Model
Portfolio-first, not asset-first.

## 4. Onboarding & Portfolio Formation
Phone number → Questionnaire (Persian) → Target allocation → Consent → Initial investment.

## 5. Asset Universe
Currency: IRR
Assets: USDT, IRR Fixed Income, Gold, BTC, ETH, QQQ, SOL, TON.

## 6. Cash Wallet
Cash is explicit and counted as Foundation.

## 7. Core Actions
Add Funds, Buy/Sell, Rebalance, Protect, Borrow.

## 8. Decision Boundary Layer
SAFE, DRIFT, STRUCTURAL, STRESS.

## 9. Stress Mode
Visible UI toggle to simulate crisis conditions.

## 10. Execution Pattern
Preview → Friction → Confirm → Commit.

## 11. Event Ledger
All actions are logged immutably.

## 12. Product Claim
Structural sanity under pressure.

## 13. Status
Logic mature. UX minimal. Direction clear.
